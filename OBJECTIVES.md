# Project Objectives: DiaMenu

DiaMenu is designed to bridge the gap between medical necessity and daily lifestyle for Filipino diabetic patients.

## 🩺 1. Clinical & Health Management
- **Glycemic Control:** Empower users to maintain stable blood sugar levels through AI-audited, low-GI meal planning.
- **Data-Driven Insights:** Provide clear visualizations of blood sugar trends linked to dietary choices to identify personal triggers.
- **Medication Adherence:** Integrate smart reminders to ensure critical medication protocols are followed.

## 🇵🇭 2. Cultural & Local Context
- **Filipino Culinary Integration:** Support authentic Filipino recipes and ingredients while making them medically safe.
- **Resource Accessibility:** Integrate local market (palengke) suggest affordable protein alternatives (e.g., tokwa, monggo).


## 🛒 3. Inventory & Smart Tracking
- **Pantry Automation:** Reduce the cognitive load of diet management by automating inventory tracking and grocery list generation.
- **Smart Auditing:** Use AI to proactively flag high-GI items in shopping lists and suggest 1-click diabetic-friendly swaps.
- **Offline Reliability:** Ensure critical meal plans and grocery lists are accessible even in low-connectivity supermarket environments.
